Our Location

Nestled in the serene and picturesque Quartier Tassoumaate, Rose Valley Hotel offers a tranquil escape with convenient access to the heart of Ouarzazate. Our ideal location combines the peace of a quiet neighborhood with the proximity to the city's famous attractions, making it the perfect base for your Moroccan adventure.
